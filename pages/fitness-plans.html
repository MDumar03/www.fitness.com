Workout Tips

Proper Warm-up

Always start with a 5-10 minute warm-up to prevent injury and prepare your body for exercise.

Stay Hydrated

Drink water before, during, and after your workout to maintain proper hydration.

Rest & Recovery

Allow adequate rest between workouts to prevent overtraining and promote muscle recovery.
